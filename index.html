Enterprise Blockchain Platforms

Capabilities - v1.0

This material is a collaborative effort to consolidate public information about Enterprise Blockchain Platforms. All information here still being validate and improved. Columns ordered by Platform name

  • Hi, everyone,

  • My name is Flavio and I hope you like this webpage about Enterprise Blockchain Platforms.

  • We plan to keep updating the content here, so visit us frequently and please, your help would be fantastic!!! You can contribute validating, fixing or adding relevant information.

  • Our goal: Give a broad view about the main DLT/Blockchain platforms being used by enterprises to build business use cases, allowing technical professionals to have very quickly, a good understanding about the main differences about these platforms, and more important, how these differences might affect or not the requirements of the business use cases considering Blockchain as the underpinning technology.

  • Roadmap (not a promise) for future versions are:

  • Build (or reference) generic use cases (equal requirements, but key to demonstrate capabilities) in different platforms and demonstrate how the requirements was affected, or not.

  • List of use cases/networks in production and their governance approach, number of transactions per day.

  • There are two ways you can contribute:

  • Via GitHub, editing the file Services.json and sending a PR.

  • ** Please, if possible, send a link related with the topic you are suggesting to change. The future idea is to have all content here reinforced with the real content (platform documentation, blog, etc)
